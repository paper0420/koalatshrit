Code of Ethics

Privacy current at 13 Nov 2023

User information

We are committed to safeguarding the privacy and confidentiality of user information. Any data collected is treated with the utmost respect for individual privacy rights. We adhere to applicable data protection laws and strive to maintain the security of user information.

Access and equity

We are dedicated to promoting access and equity in all aspects of our operations. Our services and opportunities are provided without discrimination, ensuring fair and equal access for all individuals, regardless of their backgrounds, abilities, or characteristics.

Fair practices

Our practices are guided by principles of fairness and transparency. We conduct business ethically, communicate openly with our users, and ensure that our products and services are delivered with integrity and in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

Grievance procedure

In the event of any concerns or grievances, we provide a structured grievance procedure. Users can contact our designated representative to address issues promptly and fairly. We are committed to resolving disputes in a timely and impartial manner.
