At Koala T-shirts, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

1.Information Collection:

We may collect the following types of information: Personal Information: When you create an account or make a purchase, we may collect your name, email address, shipping address, and contact details. Transaction Information: Details about the products or services you purchase from us, including payment information. We use the information for the following purposes: Order Processing: To process and fulfill your orders, send order confirmations, and manage product shipments. Communication: To send you important updates, newsletters, promotional offers, and respond to your inquiries.

We do not sell, trade, or share your personally identifiable information with third parties. Trusted partners and service providers may have access to your information solely for the purpose of assisting us in operating our website and conducting business, provided they agree to keep it confidential.

4. Controlling Personal Information with Web Security: Integrity:

We strive to ensure that your personal information is accurate, complete, and up-to-date. You can update your information in your account settings. Confidentiality: Access to your information is restricted to employees who need it for order processing, customer service, and other relevant tasks. We emphasize the importance of confidentiality to our team. Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information during transmission and storage. This includes encryption, firewalls, and regular security audits. Availability: We work to ensure that your personal information is available when you need it for legitimate purposes, such as order history and account details.

5. Policy Changes:

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices. Any modifications will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. By using our website, you consent to the terms of this privacy policy.
